Services

Physical Therapy

We provide physiotherapy, which is considered an allied health profession. It is provided by physical therapists who promote, maintain, or restore health through physical examination, diagnosis, management, prognosis, patient education, physical intervention, rehabilitation, disease prevention, and health promotion.

Speech Courses

We provide speech therapy which is the evaluation and treatment of communication problems and speech disorders. It is performed by speech-language pathologists. Speech therapy techniques are used to improve communication. These include speech therapies, language intervention activities, and others depending on the type of speech or language disorder.

Contract With Companies
